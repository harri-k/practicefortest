Rise of System-on-Chip (SoC)

  • SoC designs integrated entire systems, including processors, memory, and peripherals, into a single chip.A System-on-Chip (SoC) is an integrated circuit that combines multiple electronic components and functionalities, such as microprocessors, memory, input/output interfaces, and more, into a single chip. SoCs are commonly used in a wide range of electronic devices, including smartphones, tablets, IoT devices, and embedded systems.
