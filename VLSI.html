Introduction of VLSI

  • Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) techniques enabled the integration of thousands of transistors on a single chip.VLSI stands for Very Large-Scale Integration. It's a crucial technology and methodology in semiconductor manufacturing and electronics design. VLSI refers to the process of integrating a large number of transistors, electronic components, and functional units onto a single silicon chip or integrated circuit (IC).
