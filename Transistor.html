Invention of the Transistor

  • The transistor revolutionized electronics by providing a compact and efficient way to control electrical signals.A transistor is a fundamental semiconductor device that serves as a building block of modern electronics. It is used to amplify and switch electronic signals, making it a key component in electronic circuits. Transistors come in various types, with the most common being bipolar junction transistors (BJTs) and field-effect transistors (FETs).
