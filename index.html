• Invention of the Transistor

    The transistor revolutionized electronics by providing a compact and efficient way to control electrical signals.

  • First Microprocessor

    Intel's 4004 microprocessor marked the beginning of microcomputing, leading to the development of personal computers.

  • Introduction of VLSI

    Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) techniques enabled the integration of thousands of transistors on a single chip.

  • Rise of System-on-Chip (SoC)

    SoC designs integrated entire systems, including processors, memory, and peripherals, into a single chip.

    Other Recources

  • Nanotechnology and Beyond

    Nanotechnology pushed the limits of chip miniaturization, enabling more transistors and greater computational power.
